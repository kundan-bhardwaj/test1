Regulatory Information for Power Engineers will:
  • Broaden one's knowledge in the legislation requirements for individuals who maintain, repair and operate pressure equipment described in the Power Engineers Regulation
  • Address essential areas of the Safety Codes Act and subsidiary legislation related to the operation of pressure equipment subject to the Act
  • Streamline individual regulatory responsibilities for operation of pressure equipment
  • Support the understanding of the processes administered by ABSA
Topics include:
  • Purpose of the Power Engineers Regulation
  • The Safety Codes Act and Pressure Equipment Safety Regulation pertaining to Power Engineers
  • Supervision requirements within a plant
  • Deficiencies commonly found during plant inspections
  • Required log book entries
  • Penalties under the Power Engineers Regulation
  • Qualifications for Power Engineers
  • Duties and responsibilities of Chief Power Engineers (e.g., repairs, alterations, maintenance, training, etc.)
  • Requests for the issuance of temporary certificates
RIPE will increase awareness of what a Safety Codes Offices is reviewing when inspecting or auditing a plant.
